What is this?

so what is this? 16:th of April 2016 I wanted to create a github.io page for myself. Maybe a place to add some posts regarding thing I work on, maybe some place that I build and forget about as quickly.

So writing posts... a blogging system? A great opportunity to learn reactJs better! 30 minutes of googling and npm packages later and I had a page up and running. The problem? I did understand half of it. There was just loads of "smart" crap everywhere. So 20 minutes more of trying it out and then I finaly removed the entire page. Thought, ok, maybe not react this time. But this is a great opportunity to dive into Angular 2! 30 minutes later and I had a working angular 2 application, great! But loads of complexity and frameworking, and other stuff...

I thought to myself, what was it I wanted from the start? What do I really need? Well I need a router. 10 minutes of googling and nothing that felt just right. So I thought to myself what I great opportunity to, build my own router! I should be easy of course, super easy and it should just work so I'm thinking convention based. 30 minutes of googling, and trying out hashbang strategy and the history API with pushState and then back to hashbang again and then congratulations sir, it's a router!

30 minutes more of bugfixing and feature adding (caching is nice!) and it's a better but slightly more complex router...

30 minutes more... child view rendering would be nice... so add that... more complexity... crap
